PRIVACY POLICY

How we collect, use, and protect your information.

Last updated: January 1, 2025

SoleStore LLC ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or make a purchase.

1. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

  • Personal Information: name, email address, phone number, shipping address
  • Order Information: products purchased, order value, payment method (COD or card)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, device information via cookies
  • Usage Data: pages visited, products viewed, time spent on site

2. HOW WE COLLECT IT

  • When you place an order or create an account
  • When you contact us via our contact form or email
  • Automatically through cookies and analytics tools when you browse our site
  • When you subscribe to our newsletter

3. HOW WE USE YOUR DATA

  • To process and fulfill your orders (including Cash on Delivery coordination)
  • To communicate about your order status and shipping
  • To respond to customer service inquiries
  • To send marketing emails (only with your explicit consent)
  • To improve our website and product offerings
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. CASH ON DELIVERY ORDERS

For COD orders, we share your delivery address and order details with our third-party delivery partners solely to fulfill your order. We do not share financial information as no card payment is processed.

5. DATA SHARING

We do not sell your personal data. We may share it with: shipping and logistics partners, email service providers, payment processors (for card orders), and analytics providers. All third parties are contractually obligated to protect your data.

6. YOUR RIGHTS

  • Right to access the personal data we hold about you
  • Right to correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to deletion ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to opt out of marketing communications
  • Right to data portability

To exercise these rights, email us at help@solestore.site.

7. DATA RETENTION

We retain customer data for 5 years following your last order to comply with tax and accounting obligations, unless you request earlier deletion.

8. COOKIES

See our Cookie Policy for full details on cookie usage.
