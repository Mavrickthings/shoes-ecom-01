OUR STORY

BUILT FOR PEOPLE WHO TAKE SHOES SERIOUSLY

SoleStore was founded in 2018 in Philadelphia, PA, by a group of sneaker obsessives who were tired of paying markup prices for inferior products. We set out to build something different: a curated, honest, no-nonsense shoe store that treats customers like adults.

We stock only authentic products — verified before they ever reach your door. Every pair we carry has been selected by people who actually wear and care about footwear, not buyers chasing trending keywords.

Our Cash on Delivery option was one of our first big decisions. We believe you shouldn't have to hand over your card details online for something as personal as shoes. Pay when it's in your hands. That's how it should be.

CASH ON DELIVERY

PAY WHEN IT ARRIVES

We introduced Cash on Delivery because we think it's the most honest way to buy online. You don't pay until your shoes are physically in your hands. No trust issues. No card fraud risk. Just a straightforward exchange: shoes arrive, you pay.

COD is available on all US domestic orders. Simply select it at checkout and have the exact amount ready for the delivery driver. It's that simple.
