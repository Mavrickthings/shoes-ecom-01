WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help websites function properly, remember your preferences, and provide analytics data to site owners.

HOW WE USE COOKIES

SoleStore uses cookies to maintain your shopping cart, remember your consent preferences, store blog posts for our admin system, and analyze how visitors use our site.
